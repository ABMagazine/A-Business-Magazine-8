About Us

A Business Magazine

Who Are We


We are a group of students from the Accountancy, Business and Management strand who wish to provide the readers with a new perspective on certain issues and topics that are relevant to the present day. We came together as a class to compile our outputs together on one platform and show it to those who are interested.

Our Vision


A Business Magazine promises to deliver only the best outputs to the reader and provide timely information on many different topics. We aim to cover as many topics as possible to offer a wide variety of knowledge to those who view our online magazine.

Our Approach


Through this online magazine, we wish to provide the readers and viewers with useful and updated information in the form of different types of outputs such as articles, infographics and advertisements.
